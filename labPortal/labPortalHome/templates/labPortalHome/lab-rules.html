Lab Rules to follow in all labs

  • Student must do entry in the Lab Log book register
  • Student must do all entries correctly in the logbook, i.e. sn, system name, sap id, student name and sign
  • Student must forword the logbook to the next student after completing his entry
  • Students must complete the logbook entries in the first hour of lab time
  • Student must ensure of doing entry in logbook before leaving the lab
  • It's students whole responsibilty to do entry in lab logbook

  • Student must start the computer only after instructed by the faculty
  • Student must shutdown the computer properly before leaving the lab
  • Student must switch off the power buttons after shutting down the computer
  • Student must arrange the chair before leaving the lab
  • Student must handle the computers carefully
  • Student must not move or bend computer angle and position
  • Student must not put any thing on the computer table except pen, pencil and notebook
  • Student must keep their bags at one place in the lab instucted by the faculty
  • Student must keep water bottle down the computer table
  • The computer only have four connections, usb keyboard, usb mouse, power adapter and network port
  • Student must get familiar with computer connections
  • If computer is not starting then check for computer connections, else inform the lab assistant or faculty
  • If student find anything unusual with the computers, immediately inform to the lab assistant or faculy

  • Student must not bring eatables in the lab
  • Student must not make the lab untidy by throwing paper, pen, pencil etc in the lab
